ARCHAE EDITIONS is devoted exclusively to the works of a single author, Richard Kostelanetz. Created in response to well-known limitations in the traditional channels of literary communication, ARCHAE (formerly RK), first of all places on sale mint-condition works of his that are no longer available from their initial publishers--sometimes at remainder prices, other times at prices they would have if published anew today, and, if copies are scarce, at prices in truth essentially for archives. It also collects privately produced editions and from time to time also issues new books, audiotapes, films, videotapes, and holograms.
