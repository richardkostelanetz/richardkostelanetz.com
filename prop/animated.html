Proposal for a book on Animated Music

The purpose of the book is establishing, critically and historically, the presence of a previously unrecognized genre of an animated film based upon music. In most (perhaps all) cases, the music existed prior to the production of the film; so that the film represents a interpretative performance, much as a concert or record do.
