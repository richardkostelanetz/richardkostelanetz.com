  • I also own anthologies selecting from literary magazines, such as Curt Johnson’s Best Little Magazine Fiction (1970, 1971), Bill Henderson’s Pushcart annuals (1977–), and Denys Val Baker’s three anthologies from Little Reviews, respectively 1914–43, 1947–48, 1949 (1945, 1948, 1949).
  • Finally I treasure The Whole Cosmep Catalog that Dick Higgins edited and designed in 1973, including on a large format (11″ × 17″) single camera–ready pages produced by the member publishers in the Committee of Small Magazine Editors and Publishers (COSMEP). With well over two hundred uniquely designed pages, many with sample texts, others reproducing covers, this unpaginated book demonstrates the stylistic and literary richness of alternative publishing at that fertile time.
