Epiphanies Performance

A live performance / reading of several hundred of Richard Kostelanetz's "Epiphanies", which are one line stories "meant to be the encompassing climactic moments--Epiphanies, in the Joycean sense--of longer, otherwise nonexistent narratives". Here the audience is seated in a circle facing outwards at University of North Dakota, 1980.
